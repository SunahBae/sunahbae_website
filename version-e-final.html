About

By crossing the boundaries between technology and art, engineering and the humanities, I strive to design spatial experiences that resonate more deeply with people.

My research focuses on understanding how spatial experiences are formed through quantitative data. Currently, I am investigating how the shape of space in virtual exhibition environments influences visitors' emotions, using physiological data and questionnaires.
